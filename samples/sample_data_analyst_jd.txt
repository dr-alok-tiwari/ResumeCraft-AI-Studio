Job Title: Data Analyst
Company: TechCorp Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

About the Role:
We are looking for a skilled Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. You will be responsible for analyzing large datasets, creating dashboards, and providing actionable insights to business stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets using SQL and Python to uncover trends, patterns, and business insights
- Build and maintain interactive dashboards in Tableau or Power BI for stakeholders
- Perform statistical analysis including regression, clustering, and A/B testing
- Collaborate with business teams to translate data into actionable recommendations
- Develop ETL pipelines to automate data extraction and reporting processes
- Present findings and recommendations to senior management
- Monitor data quality and ensure data integrity across systems

Required Skills:
- 2-4 years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Strong SQL skills - MySQL, PostgreSQL, or similar
- Proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn)
- Experience with Tableau or Power BI for visualization
- Knowledge of statistical analysis and machine learning concepts
- Advanced Excel skills
- Strong communication and presentation skills

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Knowledge of Apache Spark or Airflow for data pipelines
- Familiarity with Git and version control
- MBA or Master's in Statistics, Data Science, or related field
