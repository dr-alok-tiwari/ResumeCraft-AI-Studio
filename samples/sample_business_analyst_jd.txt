Job Title: Business Analyst
Company: Accenture Strategy
Location: Mumbai / Hyderabad (Hybrid)

About the Role:
We are hiring an experienced Business Analyst to support strategy consulting engagements. You will work closely with client teams to understand business problems, gather requirements, and develop data-driven solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
- Elicit, document, and validate business and functional requirements from stakeholders
- Conduct gap analysis, process mapping, and root cause analysis
- Create BRDs, FRDs, use cases, user stories, and process flow diagrams
- Analyze business data using SQL and Excel to identify improvement opportunities
- Facilitate workshops and stakeholder interviews across business and technology teams
- Track project progress and report status to project managers and clients
- Support UAT planning and execution

Required Skills:
- 3-5 years of experience as a Business Analyst in IT or consulting
- Strong requirements gathering and documentation skills
- Proficiency in SQL and Excel for data analysis
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills

Preferred Skills:
- CBAP, CCBA, or PMI-PBA certification
- Experience with Tableau or Power BI
- Knowledge of SAP or Salesforce
- MBA from a reputed institution
